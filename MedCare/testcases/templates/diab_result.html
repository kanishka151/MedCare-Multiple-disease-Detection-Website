Essential Tips for Diabetes

  • Consistent Carbs: Keep carb intake steady. Monitor portions of carbs like grains and fruits to stabilize blood sugar.
  • Monitor Glucose: Check blood sugar often, especially before and after meals, to understand its impact on your body.
  • Foot Check: Daily foot inspection prevents complications. Keep feet clean, moisturized, and wear comfy shoes to avoid problems.
